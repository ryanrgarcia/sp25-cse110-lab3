New Business

Our target audience is middle-upper income adults who are car enthusiasts and enjoy the raw feeling of driving a motorvehicle.
Our goal is to tone back on all the new technology as much as possible to offer that raw driving experience, while still meeting today's standards of comfort and ease of use.
This will be a 2-door coupe that allows for the small and fun flickable feel of a lightweight car with the ability to use as a daily comfortably.
A total of four people will fit in the car including the driver.
We will be using a naturally aspirated 6 cylinder to power the car that will give it the nice and smooth powerband that NA vehicles are known for, while not being too heavy to throw the balance off of the car.
If need be, we can make the vehicle limited edition but for now, lets try to keep it as a regular manufactured car.
